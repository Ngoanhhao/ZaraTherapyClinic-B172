Disc Syndrome

Sciatica

Sciatica refers to pain that radiates along the path of the sciatic nerves, which branches from your lower back through your hips, buttocks, and down each leg.
Low back pain

80 percent of the population will experience back pain at some time in their lives. It can be an ongoing or chronic problem for many people.
Neck Pain

Neck pain affects roughly 10 percent of the world’s population. A stiff neck may develop gradually into pain not only in the neck but also in one or both arms.
Knee Pain

Knee pain is a health condition that substantially impacts quality of life. It is a common complaint that affects people of all ages.
Headaches

Most people experience a headache from time to time. With proper diagnosis and treatment, you can get rid of and prevent headaches in the future.
