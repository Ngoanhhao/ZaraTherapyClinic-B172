1. What is Neck Pain?

Your neck is a very complex structure designed to hold your head up all day. It consists of delicate muscles, tendons, ligaments, and joints. Desk jobs are more common nowadays, and prolonged sitting hours may put workers at an increased risk of developing neck pain. Neck pain can range from minor and easily ignored, to excruciating and debilitating.
