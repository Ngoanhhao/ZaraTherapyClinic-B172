About us

Our Clinic

Established in 2006 by Dr. Wade Brackenbury and a dedicated team of associates, The ZaraTherapyClinic has locations in Ho Chi Minh City, Da Nang, and Hanoi with modern facilities to provide international standards of healthcare services.


From April 6th, 2022, ZaraTherapyClinic officially become a member of FV group. It will help the community and patients access the most comprehensive medical care with international standard in Vietnam, with access to effective personalized treatment methods. Dr. Jean-Marcel Guillon, CEO of FV Hospital now becomes the Chairman and Legal person of ZaraTherapyClinic.

Our mission

Have a long-term commitment to provide excellent chiropractic care for musculoskeletal pain treatment to Vietnamese and Expatriates.

Pioneer in Chiropractic

ZaraTherapyClinic is the state-of-the-art Chiropractic, Physiotherapy Rehabilitation and Foot Care center, which provides the highest level of quality and facility, exceeding that of most similar clinics in South-east Asia.

Foreign doctors team

Our doctors from the United States, Canada, France, New Zealand and South Korea are well qualified, experienced, dedicated to the health of patients. We are among one of a few high quality medical establishments in Vietnam that regularly attracts overseas patients from Asian and Western countries.
