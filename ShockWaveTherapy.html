Shockwave Therapy

Shockwave therapy is a versatile device used in physiotherapy, sports medicine, and many more medical professions. Its main assets are fast pain relief and mobility restoration. It is an ideal therapy to speed up recovery and cure various indications causing acute or chronic pain.

How Does Shockwave Therapy Work?

Shockwave is an acoustic wave that carries high energy. The energy promotes regeneration and reparative processes of the bones, tendons, and other soft tissues. Shockwave therapy is most suited for people with chronic tendonitis such as Tennis Elbow, Achilles Tendonitis, rotator cuff, plantar fasciitis, etc. These could be as a result of sport, overuse, or repetitive strain.
